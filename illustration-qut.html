QUT EMBA 20 collection

This collection was commissioned to mark the graduation of the EMBA Cohort 20 from QUT in collaboration with MIT Sloan. I made the decision to create a generative collection of 100 pieces which would clearly symbolise the universities and their common approach to diversity. The cohort had a selection of pieces printed on aluminium and stored access to the NFTs on an Ledger drive for presentation to the university.

The collection was created much in the same way as GOAT Tribe, where items are illustrated on transparent backgrounds and layered at random using a Python script.
